Grade Gater

I worked on Grader Gater with my team members Mike Defrancesco who developed the front-end and Justin Schuster who developed the back-end. I made the auto grading software built into the website with php, which analyses python code, fixes some syntax errors, then runs it. Based on errors made by students when writing the python code, the grader duducts points from the question and overall test grade and stores it for when the student logs into their account to check.
